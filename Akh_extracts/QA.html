If I pay online how will I prove that I am the
owner of the building ?

  • Once your online payment has been
    completed and confirmed you will be sent
    a document of ownership to solidify your
    purchase.

What happens if I loose my document of
ownership before I am able to claim my
home ?

  • If this happens you will be able to easily
    get another copy of the document from
    your profile page.

How do I get my profile page ?

  • You can get to your profile page by
    logging in. Your profile page is the first
    page that pops up once you log in.

How can I get a discount on my home
purchase ?

  • There is already a discount if you are able
    to pay with out full payment option
